**Important:** You control what is saved. The extension only captures information when you click the save button on pages you are actively viewing.

## 3. Chrome Extension Data Handling

### 3.1 What the Extension Accesses

The Coreline Chrome Extension:
- Only activates when you click it on a job posting or profile page
- Reads visible content from the current page to extract job/contact information
- Sends that information to your Coreline account

### 3.2 What the Extension Does NOT Do

The extension does NOT:
- Run in the background or track your browsing
- Access pages you don't explicitly save from
- Store your login credentials for third-party sites
- Access private messages, emails, or other personal content

### 3.3 Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| "Read and change data on sites you visit" | Extract job details from pages when you click save |
| "Storage" | Save your preferences locally |
