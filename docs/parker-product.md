## 4. "Aha Moment" Identification

**The moment users go "I NEED this" is different per segment:**

### Active Job Seekers
> **Aha Moment:** "Wait, it shows me everyone I know at this company?"  
> **Trigger:** First time they save a job and see mutual connections surface  
> **Time to Aha:** ~2 minutes after first import

### Passive Professionals
> **Aha Moment:** "I haven't talked to [important recruiter] in 8 months?!"  
> **Trigger:** Relationship timeline shows how long since last contact  
> **Time to Aha:** ~5 minutes after importing LinkedIn contacts

### Network Builders
> **Aha Moment:** "Finally, a system that remembers what I forget."  
> **Trigger:** Searching notes and finding context from 6 months ago  
> **Time to Aha:** ~1 week (after using notes feature regularly)

### Career Insurance
> **Aha Moment:** "I actually have a pipeline of recruiters now."  
> **Trigger:** Seeing their recruiter contacts organized with specialties/notes  
> **Time to Aha:** ~10 minutes after initial setup

### Universal Aha Triggers
- First one-click import (vs. manual spreadsheet entry)
- First "you know someone" connection surfaced
- First reminder that saves them from ghosting someone
- First time they search and find an old note instantly
